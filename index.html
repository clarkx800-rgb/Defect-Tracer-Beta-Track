SUB TO SUB AREA:

📋 Welcome to Defect Tracker V2

Step 1: Start a New Route

  • Tap the Menu button at the top right.
  • Select 1. Area, 2. Direction, and 3. Sub-to-Sub Block.
  • Tap Load Sections to generate your track blocks.

Step 2: Log Defects & Take Photos

  • Scroll to your current Track Section (TS).
  • Select the Component and the Defect.
  • Tap 📷 Photo to open your camera and attach a picture.

Step 3: Save Your Progress! ⚠️

  • Open the Menu and tap 💾 Save Work frequently. This saves a backup to your phone.
  • If you close the app, tap 📂 Load Work and select your file to pick up right where you left off.

Step 4: Export Final Report

  • When your shift is done, tap 📊 Export Final Report to download your Excel spreadsheet and PDFs.
