STS:

SYSTEM INITIALIZED

STEP 1: ESTABLISH ROUTE

  • Open the top right menu.
  • Select 1. AREA, 2. DIRECTION, and 3. SUB-TO-SUB BLOCK.
  • Tap INITIATE SECTIONS to generate your track blocks.

STEP 2: LOG DEFECTS

  • Scroll to current Track Section (TS).
  • Select COMPONENT and DEFECT from dropdowns.
  • Tap 📷 PHOTO to attach visual evidence.

STEP 3: SAVE PROTOCOL ⚠️

  • System does not auto-save. Open menu and tap 💾 SAVE DATA frequently.
  • If system restarts, tap 📂 LOAD DATA to resume.
